## Required Behavior

- Create complete working code, not half-finished demos.
- Preserve direct opening of `public/index.html`.
- Preserve EN/RU/KK translations.
- Keep the app offline-first and dependency-free.
- Protect privacy and do not add tracking.
- Avoid unsafe `innerHTML` with user input.
- Escape or safely render user input.
- Keep documentation updated.
- Validate before finishing.
